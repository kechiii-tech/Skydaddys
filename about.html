Our Story

From Nairobi with code and passion

Building the Future of Web Development

Skydaddy was founded by Kipkoech Emmanuel, a Computer Science student based in Nairobi, who recognized the growing need for professional web development services in Kenya and across Africa.

What started as a solo freelance venture in 2023 has evolved into a full-service web development company serving clients locally and internationally.

Our name "Skydaddy" represents our commitment to building solutions that help businesses reach new heights in the digital space. We believe in creating digital experiences that soar above expectations.
